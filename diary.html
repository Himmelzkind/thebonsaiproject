DIARY

Day 142

15 May '21

At first it looked quite good and my bonsai recovered well from the pruning. However, for about a week now it has been looking a bit pale and losing its leaves.I hope this is just the "winter foliage" and he recovers well.

Day 94

30 March 2021

So, today was the day when I used the scissors for the first time.... I only cut back the new shoots to two leaves. It's still too early for the rest, it's just a maintenance cut. Then I gave it a little water with liquid fertiliser. Was I quite nervous? Oh yes. Does my bonsai look pretty bare now? Yup. Am I worried if it will survive? Definitely.

Day 93

29 March 2021

During the winter, my bonsai stood nice and cool and dark and obviously survived the dark season well. Since December, many new shoots have appeared despite the winter break. And it looks pretty wild by now! Maybe it's time for a first pruning...?
